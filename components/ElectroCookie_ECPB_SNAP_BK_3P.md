# ElectroCookie PCB Prototype Board

![Boards](/images/ElectroCookie_ECPB_SNAP_BK_3P.png)

I like these proto-boards versus some of the others I have tried 
due to the underside having 3 holes attached togather. This makes it much 
easier to attach components without needing to create solder bridges manually
across holes. Additionally there are  rails on two sides allowing you to cut 
down on the amount of power and ground jumpers needed. 

I picked these up on [Aamazon](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B08151V9TS?th=1) 
for `$9.99` for 3 boards. Each board has v-cut lines making it very easy 
to split it into quarters, allowing you to get 12 separate projects out 
of a single pack. This results in per unit cost of a `$0.83`.
